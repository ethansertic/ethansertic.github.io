About Ethan Sertic

My name is Ethan Sertic and I am a student at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln. I am from Franklin, Wisconsin near Milwaukee and my major is Sports Media & Communications. Sports have had a huge impact on my life and who I am today. I’ve played and watched many different sports and realized that I want to pursue a career involved in the sports field. Throughout my education and work experiences, I have found that I am happiest and perform at my best when I get to talk and watch sports which has led to my interest in sports media. I am continuing to learn new, different things in college and I’m excited for what the future holds. Continuing my education is important in order to get ready for the next part of my life after school and finding a job. If I continue to work hard and pursue my dreams, I know I’ll be able to find a job in the sports field that I will enjoy.
